    Verifiable 🔍 credentials form the backbone of Self-Sovereign Identity systems. These digital certificates contain tamper-proof 🛡️ claims about an individual's identity, qualifications, or attributes. For instance, a university can issue a digital diploma that students can share with employers. First, the issuer digitally signs the credential using cryptographic keys. Then, the credential is stored in the holder's digital wallet.📲 When needed, the holder can present these credentials to verifiers who can instantly confirm their authenticity.
