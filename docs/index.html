    What Are Verifiable Credentials (VC)? Verifiable Credent... 2024-08-12 zetrix_official
    What Are Verifiable Credentials (VC)? Verifiable Credentials (VC) are cryptographically protected digital documents that securely authenticate identity, qualifications, and rights. Built on standards like the W3C's VC Data Model, they ensure privacy, security, and trust in digital interactions. How it can be used: - Identity Verification: Replace traditional methods (usernames/passwords) to enhance security and reduce fraud. - Credential Verification: Streamline verification of qualifications (diplomas, certifications) in hiring and education. - Access Control: Improve security for accessing sensitive information (healthcare records, financial transactions). - User Consent Management: Simplify consent verification in data sharing, ensuring compliance and transparency. - E-commerce Trust: Enhance trust between buyers and sellers by verifying seller credentials or product authenticity.
